STEPS 2-3a — justapr.cu, PLAIN λ=0.10 + legacy init (the 07-06 regime):
  ALL FOUR 16-bit variants FAIL CATASTROPHICALLY at K=96.
    fp16-noref: 1,527,226 zeroed solves; RMSE explodes
    bf16-noref: 9,828,338 zeroed; train inf
    bf16+IR:    2,408,743 zeroed; NaN from iter 5
    fp16+IR:    2,433,334 zeroed; explodes
  Cause: κ(A) ≈ ||A||/λ ~ 1e5-1e6 for heavy entities under plain λ=0.1 →
  16-bit factorization loses positive-definiteness; u16·κ >> 1 so one IR
  step cannot contract. THE 16-BIT SOLVER IS DEAD IN THE PLAIN-λ REGIME.

        movies) whose dominant factor components overflow fp16 at low K.
